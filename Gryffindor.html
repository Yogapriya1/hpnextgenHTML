About:
1. Gryffindor is one of the four Houses of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and was founded by Godric Gryffindor.
2. A few known heads of Gryffindor house are Godric Gryffindor,Muldoon Cragg, Uric Oapberg, Podric Piles, Tobias Stump, Valeria Myriadd, Minerva McGonagall and Neville Longbottom.
3. The most famous possesion of Gryffindor is the Gryffindor sword.

House colours - Scarlet and Golden
Mascot - Lion
